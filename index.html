‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Is SolSnipe free to use?
A: Yes! SolSnipe is completely free. The app is supported by non-intrusive ads.
Q: Do I need to create an account?
A: No. SolSnipe does not require any account registration or login.
Q: Does SolSnipe execute trades?
A: No. SolSnipe is an analysis and research tool only. It does not execute trades, hold funds, or connect to wallets.
Q: What is the Sniper Score?
A: The Sniper Score is a 0-100 rating that evaluates token safety based on liquidity, market cap, volume, price action, buy pressure, age, contract security, and holder distribution.
Q: How does Whale Watcher work?
A: Whale Watcher tracks tokens with high net buying volume (accumulation). It shows buy pressure, net volume, and holder risk to help you spot what smart money is buying.
Q: What data does SolSnipe collect?
A: We collect minimal anonymous data (crash reports, ad tracking if you consent). We do NOT collect names, emails, wallet addresses, or any personally identifiable information.
Q: Where does the data come from?
A: We aggregate real-time data from DexScreener, Pump.fun, Raydium, Birdeye, and GeckoTerminal.
