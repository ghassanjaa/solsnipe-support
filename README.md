## ‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

### **General Questions**

**Q: Is SolSnipe free to use?**  
A: Yes! SolSnipe is completely free. The app is supported by non-intrusive ads.

**Q: Do I need to create an account?**  
A: No. SolSnipe does not require any account registration or login.

**Q: Does SolSnipe execute trades?**  
A: No. SolSnipe is an analysis and research tool only. It does not execute trades, hold funds, or connect to wallets.

---

### **Features**

**Q: What is the Sniper Score?**  
A: The Sniper Score is a 0-100 rating that evaluates token safety based on 8 factors: liquidity, market cap, volume, price action, buy pressure, age, contract security, and holder distribution.

**Q: How does Whale Watcher work?**  
A: Whale Watcher tracks tokens with high net buying volume (accumulation). It filters for tokens with active trading and displays buy pressure, net volume, and holder risk.

**Q: What is Paper Trading?**  
A: Paper Trading lets you practice trading with a simulated $10 SOL balance. It tracks your performance and displays your rank on the global leaderboard.

**Q: What sources does SolSnipe use?**  
A: We aggregate data from DexScreener, Pump.fun, Raydium, Birdeye, and GeckoTerminal for comprehensive coverage.

---

### **Privacy & Security**

**Q: What data does SolSnipe collect?**  
A: SolSnipe collects minimal data:
- **Ad Tracking**: Anonymous advertising identifiers for personalized ads (you can opt-out in Settings)
- **Analytics**: Crash reports and anonymous usage statistics to improve the app
- **No Personal Data**: We do NOT collect names, emails, wallet addresses, or any personally identifiable information

**Q: Does SolSnipe connect to my wallet?**  
A: No. SolSnipe never requests wallet connections or private keys.

**Q: Is my data shared with third parties?**  
A: Only anonymous advertising data is shared with AdMob (Google) for ad delivery. No personal information is ever sold or shared.

---

### **Technical Issues**

**Q: The app crashes on launch. What should I do?**  
A: Try these steps:
1. Force close the app
2. Restart your device
3. Check for app updates in the App Store
4. If issue persists, email us with your iOS version

**Q: Token data is not loading. What's wrong?**  
A: This is usually due to:
- Poor internet connection
- API rate limiting (wait a few minutes)
- Token not found in data sources

**Q: Scores seem inaccurate. Why?**  
A: Token scores depend on available data. Newly launched tokens may have incomplete data. Always do your own research before investing.
